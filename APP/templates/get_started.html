Create Contest

Ready to Create Your Contest? 🏆

Creating your contest is a breeze! Follow these steps:

1) Click on "Create Contest" 🚀

Begin by clicking on "Create Contest." present in the top Nav bar It's your gateway to crafting an exciting competition for others to join.

2) Fill Out the Form 📝

You'll find a form with various fields, each serving a unique purpose:

Title 🔖

Describe your contest in a nutshell. This title will be displayed on the contest cards, so make it catchy!

Description 📜

Provide a general description of your contest. Tell others who you are and what your contest is all about. Paint a clear picture for potential participants.

Fee 💰

Set the entry fee that other players will need to pay to join your contest.

Category 🏷️

Select the category your contest falls under. This helps users easily find and join contests that interest them.

Requirements 🧐

Define the requirements that participants must meet. For example, specify the required level in a game like "Town Hall 12" in Clash of Clans, or the technical skills like "ReactJS proficiency" for a coding contest. This ensures the right players join your contest.

Referral ID 🔄

Choose "Yes" if you want to use referrals and offer a 50% discount to hosts, or select "No" if you prefer not to use this feature.

3) Click "Submit" 🚀

Once you've filled out all the details, hit the "Submit" button to create your contest.

4) Pay and Earn Double 💸

To register the contest, you'll need to make a payment using either BANK or UPI. Here's the exciting part: You'll earn double the entry fee! For instance, if you set the fee at $10, you'll earn $20. Get ready to host an amazing contest and watch participants flock to your competition. Let the games begin! 🎮🎉

How contests get starts

Determining the Contest Winners 🏆

Here's how we ensure fairness and transparency in deciding the winners:

1) Contest Chat Page 📱

As soon as you create or join a contest, a dedicated chat page is created. You, your opponent, and our trusty admin will be in this chat.

2) Admin's Guidance 🧑‍💼
  • Our admin plays a crucial role. They'll assign a common task to both you and your opponent, tailored to the contest details.

  • You can count on our admin to guide you through all the necessary details and criteria.
3) Task Completion 🏁
  • Once you and your opponent have completed the task, it's evaluation time.

  • Your performance and your opponent's performance will be carefully assessed.
4) Announcing the Winner 🥇

The winner will be announced right there in the chat, and the prize money will be swiftly transferred as per your specified method.

5) Clear Reasons 📢

Rest assured, our admin will provide valid reasons for their selection, ensuring transparency and fairness.

So, get ready to bring your A-game, complete the tasks, and let the contests begin! May the best contestant win! 🎉👏
